Short Bio

I am a second-year M.S. student of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, Riverside, majored in Computer Engineering including hardware and software studies. My research and study spans Software Testing and Verification, Scientific Computing, Embedded System, Computer Architecture and IC Design and Manufacturing. Also could applied into fields as Autonomous/Unmanned Vehicles, Intelligent Transportation, Cyber-Physical Systems etc.  

YiJing is a responsible, outstanding leader and a talented engineer. He has shown his excellence during the graduate program and is graded as one of the best student. He has strong study and experiment ability, solid analytical and problem solving skills in both hardware and software, and is enthusiastic for electronics.  

YiJing completed his Bachelor of Science in Microelectronics at Fudan University (FDU), studied IC Design and Embedded Systems. He is a devoted problem solver and puts all his effort into the task, willing to learn and try new techniques for solving the problem. He has been worked as a research engineer in one national lab in university, as a member of digital system development and hardware bring-up team. He has various experiences in ASIC/FPGA Verilog design, digital system bring-up, digital signal/image processing, C/C++ programming and testing. He is familiar with processors, FPGAs, IPs, tool flows and shared infrastructure for FPGA and ASIC synthesis and design.  

He also stayed and worked on SoC (System-on-Chip) FPGA based Multimedia System as well as a Whack-a-mole Game System Design and Implementation at State Key Laboratory of ASIC and System with Professor Jinmei Lai in the FUDAN-XILINX FPGA group after graduation from FDU.
